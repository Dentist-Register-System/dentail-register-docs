# Appointment Reschedule

Purpose: Move a patient to a replacement appointment without losing history.

Key Rules:
- Reschedule reason required; note optional.
- Creates a new appointment request.
- Doctor, date and time may change.
- Replacement requires approval.
- Original appointment remains active until replacement approved.
- Original appointment transitions to Rescheduled.
- Pending reschedule may be abandoned.
- Full audit linkage retained.
